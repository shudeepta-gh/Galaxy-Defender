# Galaxy-Defender

Concept: A survival game where the player pilots a spaceship to defend against incoming waves of asteroids and enemy drones.

Shudeepta Roy Mou: The Player & Controls

Spaceship construction and Spaceship Movement Controls: Creating the spaceship model and implementing keyboard inputs that allow the ship to move smoothly up, down, left, and right within the screen boundaries.

Shooting Mechanism and Flare functionality: Spaceship shooting mechanism of the spaceship against all kinds of enemy. Flare functionality so that the spaceship can survive from the deadly red missiles up to 3 times by using this mode.

Spaceship camera setup: First Person POV and Third Person POV and Camera Movement


Brinto Das: The Enemies & Animation

Enemy spaceship Spawning and movement: Create a system that automatically generates enemies at random positions in the far distance, ensuring they don't all appear in the same spot.

Enemy shooting and collision: 2 kinds of enemies will shoot missiles . One kind(spaceship looking) will shoot blue missiles which will be randomly from which the spaceship has to survive by moving. And another type will shoot red missiles aiming at the spaceship. If the red kinds hit then the spaceship will lose lives.

Asteroid movement and collision and functionality: After scoring 150 different kinds of rock solid asteroids will come towards the spaceship. To survive this the spaceship has to move.



Nafizul Sadik: BlackHole,cheatmode and powerups

Black Hole spawning and functionalities:  Randomly at different places blackhole will appear. If the spaceship gets close to it then black hole will try to pull the spaceship towards it. Once the spaceship inside the game is over.

CHEAT MODE: Once this mode is on, the player won't be needed to do anything the spaceship will automatically kill the enemies. And the ammo will be in infinity mode. No powerups needed.

COLLECTING Powerups: Once the spaceship has finished killing enemies right at that place a green ammo will appear and the spaceship will have to collect it which will give the spaceship 20 extra ammo and 2 lives as a reward.
